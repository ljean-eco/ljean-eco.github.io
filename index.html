Léo Jean Leo Jean L. Jean

I am a postdoctoral researcher at CIRED, working with Adrien Fabre. I defended my PhD at the Paris School of Economics (PSE) in May 2026, under the supervision of Fanny Henriet (CNRS, AMSE).

My research focuses on climate policy and the oil sector, and on how heterogeneous resources, infrastructure, and ownership structures shape the path of carbon mitigation. I like to combine theoretical models with field-level data on oil extraction.

During my PhD I spent several stays abroad as a visiting researcher. I was at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in 2024 and again in 2026, working with Julien Daubanes, and at McGill University in 2025, working with Hassan Benchekroun.

I will be on the 2026/2027 job market for academic and research positions.
