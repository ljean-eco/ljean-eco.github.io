I am an environmental and energy economist. I recently defended my PhD at the Paris School of Economics, under the supervision of Fanny Henriet (CNRS, AMSE).

My research focuses on climate policy and the oil sector, with particular attention to how heterogeneous resources, infrastructure, and ownership structures shape the path of carbon mitigation. I combine theoretical models with field-level data on oil extraction.

I am currently a visiting researcher at the Technical University of Denmark (January–June 2026), working with Julien Daubanes.

My thesis, Essays on Climate Policy and Heterogeneous Oil Resources, was defended on 29 April 2026.
